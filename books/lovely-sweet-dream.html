Lovely Sweet Dream

Lovely Sweet Dream is a book that was released simultaneously with LSD: Dream Emulator and LSD and Remixes, along with the Lucy in the Sky with Dynamites CD. It was written over the course of ten years by Hiroko Nishikawa, an artist for Asmik Ace and Osamu Sato's wife.
